Gomti Udgam Sthal



Gomti Udgam Sthal




About


Aarti is performed at Gomti Udgam Sthal every evening just like in Varanasi Believed to be a divine river, Gomti is one of the many tributaries of the holy river Ganges. According to vedic text and Hindu mythology, this river is the daughter of Rishi Vashist, one of the Saptarishis.



Gomti, one of the holy rivers of India and lifeline of Uttar Pradesh, originates from Gomat Taal, Fulhaar Jheel in Madho Tanda, Pilibhit. It travels around 47 kms in the district and then moves to other parts of UP, including Jaunpur, Lakhimpur Kheri, Sitapur and the capital city Lucknow.
